The Vibranium Dome Dashboard is the most important analytics page and it is the web application landing page.
It is designed to answer the most cruisial questions by a quick look
- LLM usage timeline
- What LLM Agents or applications are currently running in my organization?
- What models are being used, what is the proportion out of the entire usage?
- Risk and alerting usage interactions
- filter interactions by user, ip, session-id etc

The dashboard is the one stop shop for all of the security team needs during post mortem, search all llm interactions
and much more
