This shield finds all completion matches of a policy defined list of regular expresion, it is intended to detect sensetive data being returned by the LLM.
this vulnerability can also be triggered in case the model is pretrained with sensetice information

the patterns can find sensetive data such defined by the company for example insurance policy number in an insurance company
the goal is to detect any plain text, company defined sensetive data sent to the model
